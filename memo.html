GIDEON FOREX ACADEMY (GFA)
Investment Memorandum

1. Introduction

Gideon Forex Academy (GFA) is a financial education and trading institution specializing in forex market training and professionally managed trading services. In response to growing client demand, GFA has introduced a structured investment program designed to provide participants with the opportunity to earn consistent returns through professionally executed trading strategies.

This Investment Memorandum outlines the structure, terms, and conditions governing participation in the GFA Investment Program. All investments are managed under strict principles of transparency, prudent risk management, and financial integrity.

2. Investment Overview

  • Minimum Investment: USD 3,000
  • Program Manager: Mr. Omoit Gideon Michael (CEO, GFA)
  • Minimum Investment Duration: 12 months
  • Target Annual ROI: Approximately 100% (based on market performance)
  • Profit Sharing: Determined by investment type and tier (see below)

3. Investment Options

A. Pooled Account (Minimum Investment: USD 3,000)

  • Investor funds are combined into a managed trading pool operated by GFA.
  • Profit Distribution: 10% monthly to the investor, with the option to compound for higher returns.
  • Reporting: Monthly or quarterly profit updates provided.
  • Withdrawals: 10% monthly payment to investor; initial capital locked for 12 months unless otherwise agreed.

B. Individually Managed Account (Investment of USD 5,000 & above)

  • Investor opens a personal trading account with a regulated broker.
  • Investor maintains full ownership and control; GFA is granted trade-only access (no withdrawal rights).
  • Profit Sharing: 40% to the investor, 60% to GFA. Profits are shared monthly or annually as agreed.
  • Reporting: Monthly performance reports provided.
  • Investor can monitor account performance at any time.

4. Risk Management & Termination Clause

Forex trading involves significant financial risk, including the potential for partial or total capital loss. GFA employs disciplined trading strategies and advanced risk management tools, including stop-loss protocols.

  • Capital Loss Tolerance: If cumulative trading losses exceed 40% of invested capital, the investor reserves the right to immediately terminate the investment agreement and withdraw remaining funds.
  • Upon termination under this clause, the investor incurs no further obligations.
  • GFA will promptly notify investors if losses approach the stated threshold.

5. Fees & Profit Distribution

  • No upfront management fees charged.
  • Profit-sharing applies only to realized net gains.
  • Profit distributions may be made quarterly or annually, per investor preference.
  • A formal Investment Agreement defines profit calculation, timelines, and payment procedures.

6. Account Setup Process

  1. Consultation: Align investment goals, risk appetite, and strategy preferences.
  2. Legal Documentation: Sign the Investment Agreement and Risk Disclosure Form.
  3. Funding:
    • Pooled Accounts: Investor transfers funds to GFA‚Äôs designated trading pool.
    • Individually Managed Accounts: Investor funds and controls the account; grants trade-only access to GFA.
  4. Trading Commencement: GFA begins trading operations; performance updates shared monthly.

7. Exit Terms

  • Pooled Accounts: Full capital withdrawal permitted after 12 months; partial withdrawals available thereafter.
  • Individually Managed Accounts: Investors may close or withdraw funds anytime, subject to broker conditions.
  • Immediate termination rights apply if losses exceed 40% of initial capital.

8. Legal & Compliance

GFA adheres to international Know-Your-Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations. Each investor will receive a detailed Risk Disclosure Statement prior to participation.
